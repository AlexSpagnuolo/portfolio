Tent Cards

April 2019

Introducing kombucha readily on tap. This tent card series brings advertising to the personal level of being on consumers' table.

Challenge

Design two tent cards informing that kombucha is available on tap and bottles to purchase.

Goal

Reach new customers and capture new markets. Apply the already established feel of the brand to this marketing channel to improve customer acquisition.

Client

OK Kombucha

Fields

Print, typography

Software

Final Product

This creative translation of OK Kombucha's core messaging and visual expression catches the eyes of whoever sits infront of them. Tent cards provide small, yet mighty, innovative growth opportunities.
