LOTBOT

LotBot is a personable and friendly app that sorts and assigns parking spots daily. Keeping in mind user time frame needs, and considering accessibility, all users will get a spot efficiently. A team project with Alexandra Mines and Raquel Alves.

Fields

App design, UI/UX, branding, visual identity, icon design

Roles

Team Lead, production artist

Software

Challenge

A second year college study. Regular Algonquin College parkers want to find a spot efficiently because their time is restricted.

Algonquin College parking lot

Goal

A second year college study. Design an iOS app which solves the issues of parking at Algonquin College.

Process

Conduct competitive analysis, conduct user interviews, paper prototype, create wireframes, test usability

Final Product

The target audience will be interested in LotBot because it eases the mind of drives by completely removing the stress of finding a parking spot. It efficiently assigns spots to maximize the usability of every lot. Our designs and bot character humanize the app, giving us an edge over competitors, with accessibility kept in mind.

I learned some key lessons about designing for real people.
Everyone has different needs and in order for an app to survive and rise in the market, it must consider and support all accessibility needs.
Step out of my comfort zone and get real people to test and ask questions.
