Final Annual Report

April 2017

The World Animal Protection’s vision is a world where animal welfare matters and animal cruelty has ended. This annual report serves to show their goals while marketing their brand.

Challenge

The organization needed a robust and clean annual report to properly demonstrate their brand and company values.

Goal

Create an annual report to show the organization’s identity and brand as a marketing opportunity. Boost shareholder confidence and cause more investors to gravitate toward the company. Help investors and other readers keep the company in mind long after they finish reading it.

Client

World Animal Protection

Fields

Print, typography

Software

Final Product

The New Moon Tattoo logo reflects a clean and straight forward approach that customers can come to appreciate and positively reflect a body mod parlour. The fluid structure of the drips resemble ink and is clear no matter the perspective. It reflects the diversity and all the identities working at the parlour.

New Moon Tattoo has a lot of personality in terms of the individuality of each artist. A uniform and organized identity for them to work alongside is now provided.
