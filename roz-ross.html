Roz Ross
Executive Coaching

December 2018

A fluid brand and platform that allows Ross to challenge clients to co-create a future that inspires them. A team project of three others including me as lead.

Challenge

Roz was starting her executive coaching business and needed a brand and website that successfully reflected her as a person and a professional.

Goal

Oversee a team effort in designing and developing a visual identity for Roz while addressing the professionality of her field and her target market. Design a brand guide and code a website.

Client

Roslynn Ross

Fields

Branding, web

Software

Process

Final Product

A fully functioning website created with Jekyll and NetlifyCMS in order for the client to easily customize was produced. The website features the introductory home page, an about page, all the offered services, and a blog page. The other two members of my team designed a brand guide which I oversaw.

Our work as a team was featured in the Algonquin College Office of Applied Research, Innovation & Entrepreneurship's applied research showcase, RE/ACTION. On December 4th, 2018 many people visited and engaged with over 80 research teams, where we showcased our findings and creations.
