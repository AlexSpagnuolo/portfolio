Photography

Photography as a hobby of mine allows me to express my personal aesthetic style. My photos range from brutalist architectural photos hightlighting detail in dynamic and different angles and crops, to the aggressive, bold, and revealing use of the flash on the human body.

The ultimate goal of my photography is to get the viewer to question. Question everything:
What you see,
what you hear,
what you feel.

My art isn't necessarily supposed to look nice or make sense, but rather make you feel something. Inspire something. Provoke certain thoughts. This is a collage of some of my favourite pieces that I've shot and edited.
