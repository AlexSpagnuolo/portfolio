A tattoo and piercing shop, now with a uniform identity to allow the art created within to shine. Check out New Moon Tattoo. fields: Branding, print design, typography roles: Sketch, design, edit photography, print challenge: A second year college study. Current perceptions of tattoo parlours may not be the most positive or inviting. The graphic design and branding should not lack because of the beautiful work that gets created inside. Without consistency and a clear professional message, the brand falls apart. goal: Provide a straight forward, dynamic & different brand that creates a positive perception and reflects the company’s initiatives to provide diverse work for their clients. process1: Beginning with a creative brief presentation, I conducted market research in order to find out more about the target audience, competitors, and project specifics. Four weeks of sketching logo concepts then ensued. process2: Once the look of logo was decided, the rest of the visual design fell in place. Many options while planning for the stationery package and brand guide stood out but the designs below proved to be the most effective for the business. Everything maintaining a cohesive look and construction backs up the business by providing consistency that can actually be taken seriously. finalproduct1: The New Moon Tattoo logo reflects a clean and straight forward approach that customers can come to appreciate and positively reflect a body mod parlour. The fluid structure of the drips resemble ink and is clear no matter the perspective. This helps reinforce a large facet of the business. It reflects the diversity and all the identities working at the parlour. finalproduct2: The new brand will set in motion a new welcoming and professional vibe to create the trust that customers demand in a current and well-rounded tattoo and piercing business. New Moon Tattoo has a lot of personality in terms of the individuality of each artist. A uniform and organized identity for them to work alongside is now provided. footercolourclass: newmoonfooter footertoplinkclass: whitetoplink iconcolour: text-white footerlinkclass: footerlinkwhite ---
